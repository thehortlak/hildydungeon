You look around the dungeon.

The room is circular, and the walls are a mix of dirt and stone,
with some Hildy-sized tunnels dug out around the perimeter.
It occurs to you this is probably more of an old abandoned well than a dungeon.



The torches cast a warm glow.
It's pretty cozy for a well.
A big pile of blankets in the corner must be Hildy's bed.
Toys and games and various of Hildy's treasures are scattered about.
She has some boy band posters taped up on the wall.

Suddenly, a movement across the room startles you.
Crawling out of one of the tunnels is a skeleton,
and he's coming right for you!



>HYAHH!!!!!!





Hildy jumps out and bops the skeleton on the head,
and he crumbles into an old pile of bones.
Hildy turns to check on you.



>Are you okay?
Sorry about that!
This cursed skeleton infestation is really getting out of hand.
